Non-Disclosure and Non-Compete Agreement

This Agreement is made and entered into as of the date of last signature below, by and between Dr. Dolo Dorsey, both in an individual capacity and as Founder & CEO of The Kollective Hospitality Group, including all affiliated brands, ventures, and intellectual property, collectively the Disclosing Party, and the undersigned individual, group, or entity, the Receiving Party.

1. Purpose

The Parties wish to engage in preliminary discussions relating to potential business collaborations, investments, partnerships, or other opportunities. During these discussions, the Disclosing Party may disclose strategic, creative, operational, and financial information that must remain confidential. Access is conditioned upon strict adherence to these terms.

2. Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes business models, methods, trade secrets, unreleased branding concepts, artwork, logos, mascots, marketing strategies, financial forecasts, pitch decks, revenue structures, internal processes, automations, software systems, third-party relationships, supplier/vendor/partner details, client lists, staff structures, compensation strategies, operational plans, and plans related to current or future projects, properties, restaurant concepts, product lines, or expansions under The Kollective.

3. Receiving Party Obligations

The Receiving Party agrees to treat all Confidential Information as strictly confidential, not disclose it without prior written consent, not replicate, reverse engineer, or use it for personal or commercial gain, limit access to bound need-to-know parties, and notify the Disclosing Party immediately of any breach, suspected breach, or legal requirement compelling disclosure.

4. Non-Compete and Non-Circumvention

For two years from the Effective Date, the Receiving Party shall not compete directly or indirectly with any disclosed business model, property, concept, or entity under The Kollective Hospitality Group in the same vertical or market segment; circumvent the Disclosing Party by contacting vendors, clients, employees, investors, or partners introduced by the Disclosing Party; repurpose any concept, idea, branding, system, or strategy received under this Agreement; or collaborate with third parties to execute a similar or derivative model discussed during confidential interactions.

5. Intellectual Property Ownership

All creative and strategic assets, including brand identities, logos, names, mascots, menus, events, digital products, visual presentations, content, business plans, proprietary frameworks, and verbal or written ideas disclosed during working sessions or strategic discussions remain the sole intellectual property of Dr. Dorsey and The Kollective. No rights or licenses are granted unless explicitly stated in writing.

6. Return or Destruction of Materials

Upon request, the Receiving Party agrees to return all physical materials and digital copies, permanently delete files, passwords, and records, and certify destruction or return if requested.

7. No Partnership Created

This Agreement does not create a partnership, joint venture, employment agreement, or agency relationship and is not a commitment to enter into any transaction.

8. Remedies for Breach

The Receiving Party acknowledges breach may cause irreparable harm and that the Disclosing Party may seek injunctive relief and all remedies available at law or equity, including attorney fees and costs.

9. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Agreement is governed by Texas law. Disputes shall be resolved exclusively in state or federal courts located in Harris County, Texas.

10. Term and Survival

This Agreement remains in effect for two years from the Effective Date or until terminated in writing by mutual consent. Obligations regarding confidentiality, non-circumvention, and intellectual property survive termination.

11. Entire Agreement

This document constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding the subject matter and supersedes prior communications. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both Parties.
